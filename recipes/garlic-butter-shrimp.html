Garlic Butter Shrimp

This is a classic tangy louisiana dish

whithin this dish consists four walls of bursting flavore that will have a nice initial tang within the first few seconds then comes the savery spices glazed on top of each and every perfectly prepared shrimp and then the third wall hits you when you take a bite of this localy grown lousiana shrimp that pops the garlicy brine cotting your mouth with a strong flavore almost to much .... until the shrimp comes in to even your palet with one of the tastiest protens on the planet. this dish is pure satifaction.

    Ingredients

  • Shrimp (1 lb)
  • Garlic (4 cloves)
  • Butter (4 Tbs)
  • Olive Oil (2 Tbs)
  • Salt (1/2 Ts)
  • Black Pepper (1/4 Ts)
  • Red Pepper Flakes (pinch)
  • Fresh Parsley (for garnish)
  • Lemon

    Instructions

  1. Prepare the Shrimp: Pat the shrimp dry with paper towels and season with salt and black pepper.
  2. Cook the Shrimp: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp in a single layer and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side, until pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Make the Garlic Butter Sauce: In the same skillet, add the butter and minced garlic. Cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, until the garlic is fragrant and softened. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
  4. Combine Shrimp and Sauce: Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss them in the garlic butter sauce until evenly coated. Cook for an additional minute to heat through.
  5. Serve: Transfer the garlic butter shrimp to a serving platter or individual plates. Garnish with chopped parsley, red pepper flakes (if using), and lemon slices.
  6. Enjoy: Serve the garlic butter shrimp hot, accompanied by your favorite side dishes such as rice, pasta, or crusty bread.
